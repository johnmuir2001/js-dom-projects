There are 2 versions of this game. The single player version, where the user rolls a dice and tries to get over 20. Each time the dice is rolled the number it lands on is added to a total but if a 1 is rolled then the score goes back to 0. The 2 player version is a similar concept where the player aims for a score over 20. This time you can 'hold', when a player holds the score up until then gets added to their total and then it switches to the next player. When a 1 is rolled the current score of rolls resets to 0 and its the other players go.

Simon Says Demo

The goal of blackjack is to reach 21 and beat the dealer. Before the cards are dealt you place a bet. If you win you get the amount you bet added to your total. If you lose the amount you bet gets subtracted from your total. You and the dealer are dealt 2 cards but you can only see one of the dealers cards. You then decide if you want to 'stand' and keep the cards you have, or 'hit' where you get another card. You can get as many cards as you want but you lose if you go over 21. When you 'stand' the dealer then shows their second card and keeps adding more cards if they have a total below 17. If the dealers cards add up to between 17 and 21 they can't add any more cards. If the dealer has 21 you lose, and if the dealer goes over 21 you win.
